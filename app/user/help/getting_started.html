Getting Started with XMRPool.net

If you are new to mining, this is the place for you.

take a deep breath, its only confusing at first but a joy once you figure it out.
1. Create a Wallet

Both online wallets and hardware wallets have their pros and cons.

MyMonero : MyMonero is an online wallet maintained by the core monero team, so while safe is still suceptable to hacks.

Monero GUI Wallet : The safest way to safely store and secure your XMR, although it would be your responsibility to kee your XMR Safe.
2. Download mining software

Nvidia Miner

CPU Miner (Older CPU's})
3. Pick a server and port

There are 2 important things to take a note of here the URL and the port that you are going to connect to.

In the ports list you'll notice theres Global, PPLNS, Solo (What?)

Global is simply a collection of both PPLNS & Solo ports, powerd by GEO DNS and usually the safest choice as it automagically find the closest server to you. This is also convenient as it could be used to redirect traffic to another node in case of an outage (AWS East Outage. Case & Point)

And if you prefer to shave off a few of those ms and connect directly, you have the option to select your preferred endpoint too.
